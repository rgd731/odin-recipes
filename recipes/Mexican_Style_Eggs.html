Mexican Style Eggs

Mexican Style Eggs are, as the name implies, how many people from Mexico make their scrambled eggs in the morning. It's a combination of many commonly used ingredients, and makes for a very simple and delicious breakfast.

This recipe call for:

  • 3 Eggs
  • Half a Roma Tomato
  • A Slice of Onion
  • Half a Serrano Pepper
  • A Tbsp of Canola Oil
  • A dash of salt and black pepper

This recipe is fairly simple, requiring only a few steps.

  1. Dice the roma tomato
  2. Finely chop the slice of onion.
  3. Chop the serrano pepper
  4. Heat the canola oil in a small pan (medium heat)
  5. First, add the chopped onion to the pan, cook until brown
  6. Now, add the tomato and chopped pepper, cook for about a minute
  7. After that, you add and scramble your eggs
  8. Add your salt and black pepper
  9. Finally, you continue to cook until the eggs are at your desired consistency.
